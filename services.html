Important: Dry Cut Preparation

We start with a dry cut. You must arrive with your curls down and dry. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair up, like buns or ponytails. You can use hair products as long as the hair is NOT sticky or oily. Breaking these rules might lead to rescheduling or a fee. Let's keep it hassle-free!
